Volunteering

Volunteers are an ESSENTIAL part of the inner-workings of Lovable Adoptable Pet Center, and play an important role in saving animal lives in the Chattanooga area! Volunteers are at the frontlines of helping staff to provide quality love and care for animals and helping get them into forever homes! We are always looking for passionate, dedicated volunteers to help us further our mission and can use a variety of different skills and interests!
