Creating Design for Purpose

Hello, my name is Susan Burns.
I am currently working in sales, but have become interested in Web Design, coding, and designing. I love creating other's ideas and watching them come to life through working on their websites. I not only can create a website for you, but also design logos. This passion stems from my first love of interior design. A long time ago, I worked in textiles and was able to put my interior design talents to work. When life happened, I ended up in sales. When I decided to put myself first, I went back to school and found that I am really good at coding. I hope that I help use this talent to bring your designs and ideas to life.
