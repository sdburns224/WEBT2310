About Steve's

Located on Soddy Lake in Soddy, Daisy, Tennessee, Steve’s Landing Waterfront Grill offers a variety of American cuisine from hot wings and Steve’s world famous BBQ ribs to mahi-mahi. We have an extensive American cuisine menu including other items such as hamburgers, fried shrimp, and bread pudding. We invite you to come dine in a casual, family atmosphere. Bring your best friend, we have dog friendly seating on the patio. Come by car or by boat, we have plenty of parking for both as well as water planes.
